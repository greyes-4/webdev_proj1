What is One Piece About?

One piece focuses on the the protagnist Monkey D. Luffy, a joyful young man made of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit, who goes on a journey from the East Blue Sea to find the greatest treasure known as the One Piece who was left behind the deceased King of Pirates Gol D. Roger.

Favorite Moments

Zoro vs King

Zoro, who is Luffy's right hand man, is facing off against King, Kaidos right hand man. The visuals and effects that are seen through this episodes send chills everytime. A faceoff between the right hand mans of two very powerful captains.

Luffy Transforms to gear 5

With Lucci trying to capture Luffy, Luffy introduces Lucci to his newest and strongest transformation that he has aquired!

Luffy Punches a Celestial Dragon

After Luffy's friend is shot by a Celestial Dragon, who are considered to be founders and the highest social class in the world, Luffy approaches the Celestial Dragon and punches him right in the face! This is never seen as Celestial Dragons have been seen to always be obeyed and never approached.
